Alignment		Description
Lawful Good		Can be counted on to do the right thing as expected by society.
Good			Do the best they can to help others according to their needs.
Chaotic Good	Act as their conscience directs, with little regard for what others expect.
Lawful			Act in accordance with law, tradition, or personal codes.
Neutral			Prefer to steer clear of moral questions and don't take sides, doing what seems best at the time.
Chaotic			Follow their whims, holding their personal freedom above all else.
Lawful Evil		Methodically take what they want, within the limits of a code of tradition, loyalty, or order.
Evil			Do whatever they can get away with, without compassion or qualms.
Chaotic Evil	Act with arbitrary violence spurred by their greed hatred, or bloodlust.
